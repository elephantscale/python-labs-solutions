What happened?¶

Oops! Looks like our heatmap is entirely focused on the stats for Games. That's the one with the largest magnitude. Let's try to normalize this.

Here's the formula.

(Column Val - Mean() ) / Std

You can call this on the entire colums:

(df - df.mean())/df.std()
